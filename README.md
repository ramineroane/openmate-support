# OpenMate Support
![3-together](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/981cce14-eb9c-4135-b2ac-4e4d67255cbf)

OpenMate is a fully offline chess app with no data collection.

## Features
- Strong, fast chess engine: ~4M nodes per second on an iPhone 16 (half on Low Power mode), ~2800 ELO at 3s / move
- No internet access (work son airplane mode)
- Coaching hints (openings, engine moves, threats). Learn chess openings.
- Strategy tips in plain English
- No ads, no accounts, no tracking. Enjoy!

## Privacy Policy
OpenMate does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection. No user information is transmitted to external servers.
OpenMate does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party services.
All gameplay and processing occur locally on the device.
